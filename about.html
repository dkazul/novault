ABOUT

What is NOVAULT?

NOVAULT is a game preservation project dedicated to documenting and preserving the history of classic video games and physical media.

The project focuses on preserving information that can disappear over time, including original releases, regional versions, disc data, artwork, and historical documentation.

Our Mission

NOVAULT aims to create a reliable archive focused on the preservation of gaming history, keeping records of physical releases and their technical details for future reference.

Why Preservation Matters

Games are more than software. They are cultural artifacts that represent different eras of technology, design, and entertainment. Physical releases contain details that are often forgotten over time.

What We Archive

• Physical game releases
• Disc information and technical data
• Regional editions and versions
• Packaging and artwork
• Historical documentation
